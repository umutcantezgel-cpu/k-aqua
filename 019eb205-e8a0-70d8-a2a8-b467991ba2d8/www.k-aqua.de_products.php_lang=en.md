# PP-R/PP-RCT Pipes and Fittings for drinkable hot and cold water

[![](https://www.k-aqua.de/Pics/products_kaqua_house.jpg)](https://www.k-aqua.de/Pics/products_kaqua_house_zoom.jpg)

## K-Aqua product range

The K-Aqua PP-R/PP-RCT piping system includes different types of pipes (mono layer and multilayer reinforced pipes with glass fiber) with several wall thicknesses in SDR 6, SDR 7,4, SDR9, SDR 11 and SDR 17. Depending of the pipe type the available dimensions starts from size 20 mm up to 630 mm. The fittings are available in sizes 20 mm up to 315 mm as well ascomplementary products like valves, unions, cutting tools, welding equipment, welding devices, and more...
